Lentil Stew

Description

The information and picture for this practice project is used from the following page: Lentil stew

Lentil stew is a delicious dish to eat :)

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Green Peas
  • Coconut oil
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • lemons

Steps

  1. Boil the peas in a pot of water. Keep on cooking until there is not a lot of water left in the pot.
  2. Cut the vegetables in the meantime.
  3. Add pepper and salt to flavor the dish.
