Wiener Schnitzel

Description

The information and picture for this practice project is used from the following page: Schnitzel

Schnitzel is a traditional dish in Austria.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken.
  2. Put the chicken into the flour, egg and then into the bread crumbs.
  3. Put the prepared chicken into the pan with oil.
  4. Roast the chicken and prepare side-dishes in the meantime.
