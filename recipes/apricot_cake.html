Apricot Cake

Description

The information and picture for this practice project is used from the following page: Apricot crumble cake

Apricot cake is a delicious cake in Austria.

Ingredients

  • White sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Self-rising flour
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Apricots

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Put the butter, sugar, eggs into a bowl and use an electric mixer.
  3. Pour the inside of the bowl onto a prepared baking dish and add the apricots on the top.
  4. Bake in the oven until the top is golden and the inside is not sticky anymore.
